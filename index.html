About BECIL

Broadcast Engineering Consultants India Limited (BECIL) an ISO 9001:2015, ISO 27001:2013, ISO/IEC 20000-1:2018 and CMMIL3 certified, a Mini Ratna Central Public Sector Enterprise (CPSE) was incorporated on 24th March, 1995 under the Companies Act, 2013 (erstwhile the Companies Act, 1956) by Government of India with 100% equity share capital of BECIL held by President of India through Secretary and Joint Secretary of Ministry of Information & Broadcasting.
